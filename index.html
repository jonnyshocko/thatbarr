Our Story

Born from decades of patient care

The inventor of That BARR is Dr. Ryan Anthony Swier, a licensed chiropractic physician who has been running a successful practice in St. Charles, Missouri for over 25 years. Dr. Swier comes from a family of chiropractors spanning four generations — including his uncle, great uncle, great grandfather, and several cousins.

Dr. Swier earned his Bachelor of Science in Human Biology and his Doctor of Chiropractic degree from Logan College of Chiropractic (now Logan University). He takes pride in giving exceptional care and spending time with his patients while giving them relief from their symptoms.

The idea for That BARR developed after decades of selling a popular pain relief product in his office that came in gel, roll-on, and spray options. The roll-on was the best seller because many patients didn't like the feeling of residual product on their hands. However, it was limited to certain areas of the body based on the patient's reach.

After extensive research, Dr. Swier found that no mass-produced product existed on the market to solve this problem — and That BARR was born.

🏗️

Engineered With Purpose

Designed to fit most 2.5–3 oz roll-on bottles with a tapered canister. The ergonomic curve minimizes stress on arm joints, and the I-beam design provides strength and durability with minimal flex.
